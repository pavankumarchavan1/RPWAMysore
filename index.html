RAILWAY PENSIONERS WELFARE ASSOCIATION

The Railway Pensioners Welfare Association, Mysore is for the Railway Pensioners of Mysore division to offer support and render welfare services to Railway Pensioners.

This Association was formed in a meeting held on 14.05.1995 and registered under Karnataka Cooperative Societies Act vide no.212/96-97.

Over the course of its journey, this Association has established a firm foot hold. This Association's Office bearers especially the President Sri Parthasarathy and the General Secretary Sri G.S.Swaminathan are dedicated towards their respective role and put in a lot of efforts to achieve the goal. In the near future, this Association aims to expand its services to cater to large Pensioners. Staff,' Officers and Bank Officials are cooperating in solving almost all grievances of pensioners since our relation is cordial with them.

Based on the activities of this Association, the Ministry of Pension and Pensioners Welfare New Delhi had nominated this Association as Member from 2020 to 2024 to its Standing Committee of Voluntary Agencies. This Association is also identified by the Ministry and getting Grant in Aid every year. Railway Administration has given accomodation to this Association for a rent of Rs.1000 per year.
